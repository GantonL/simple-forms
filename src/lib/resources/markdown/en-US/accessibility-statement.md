# Accessibility Statement

## Our Commitment to Accessibility

We are committed to ensuring that this application is accessible and usable by all individuals, including people with disabilities. We continuously strive to enhance the accessibility of our site and align our practices with leading global accessibility guidelines, fostering an inclusive digital environment.

## Standards and Guidelines

This application adheres to the international accessibility guidelines WCAG 2.1 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) at AA level. Where applicable, we also reference Israeli Standard IS 5568. Our ongoing efforts are dedicated to meeting and exceeding these standards.

## Accessibility Features on Our Application

### Keyboard Navigation

- The application can be fully navigated using a keyboard only.
- All interactive elements are focusable and operable via keyboard.
- Focus indicators are clear, visible, and distinct.

### Screen Reader Support

- The application is designed to be compatible with leading screen readers such as NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver.
- Images include descriptive alternative text (alt text) for better comprehension.
- Headings are structured hierarchically to provide a clear outline of content.

### Contrast and Colors

- Text-to-background contrast ratios meet or exceed WCAG AA requirements for readability.
- Information is not conveyed through color alone; alternative visual cues are provided.
- The application supports both user-selectable light and dark themes to accommodate preferences and reduce eye strain.

### Fonts and Sizes

- Clear, readable, and scalable fonts are used throughout the application.
- Text can be enlarged up to 200% using browser zoom functionality without loss of content or functionality.
- Appropriate spacing and layout ensure readability and ease of navigation.

## Known Issues and Improvement Plans

We are dedicated to ongoing accessibility improvements. User feedback is invaluable in this process. If you encounter any accessibility issues, barriers, or have suggestions for improvement, please do not hesitate to contact us.
